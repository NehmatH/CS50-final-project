Let's Meet

There's nothing better than hanging out with your friends, yet planning it is always such a hassle. From choosing a location, a date and a time that fit every invitees schedule, to making sure the selections satisfy everyone's taste, coordinating such events can take lots of time and effort, especially if someone decides to drop out at the last minute.

Well, all of that can be solved with Let's Meet. With Let's Meet, you now can:

  • Send friend requests to your friends and add them to your friends list once they accept your request.
  • Create new events and invite your friends.
  • Once you're a participant in an event, stay if you want to go or leave the event if you don't.

HOW THE WEBSITE WORKS

{% endblock %} {% block fourth %}
1. Search and Add Friends

Search users and send friend requests to your friends.

2. Pick Name and Location

Name your event and choose a location.

3. Pick Date and Time

Choose a date, a start and an end time.

4. Pick Participants

Choose participants from your friends list to invite to your event.

5. Create Event

And that's it! You can now check your event in the "My Events" page.

6. Edit event

You can add and remove participants or even delete the event.

About Us

{% endblock %} {% block sixth %}

Our mission

At Let's Meet, we believe there is a better way to planning meetings and events. A more user-friendly, less complicated way where users solve one of their most common problem the easiest way possible. This is why we work everyday in order to provide you with the fastest, simplest and most effective methods that allow you to make your plans comfortably.

One of the inspirations that led to the production of this website is always having trouble planning a meeting, where the location, date and time are compatible with every invitees schedule. Knowing that this task takes lots of efforts and requires constant communication with all the invitees, we worked at Let's Meet to find a way that reduces all the trouble and allows users to plan the event the easiest way possible.

Our founding

Let's Meet is a free and secure website created and designed by Nehmat Hatoum, a senior from Beirut, Lebanon. The website was founded in August 2020 as a final project for the online Harvard course, Introduction to Computer Science, widely known as CS50.
